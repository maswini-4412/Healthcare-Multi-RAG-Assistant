Malaria Medical Information

Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by Plasmodium parasites
transmitted through infected mosquitoes.

Symptoms:
Fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea, vomiting,
body pain, and weakness.

Treatment:
Malaria is treated using antimalarial medicines.
Treatment depends on parasite type and disease severity.

Prevention:
Use mosquito nets, insect repellents,
and preventive medicines in high risk areas.
