Dengue Fever Medical Information

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection caused by dengue virus.

Symptoms:
High fever, severe headache, pain behind eyes, muscle pain, joint pain,
skin rash, nausea, vomiting, and fatigue.

Warning signs:
Severe abdominal pain, persistent vomiting, bleeding gums,
difficulty breathing.

Treatment:
There is no specific antiviral medicine for dengue.
Treatment focuses on fluid replacement, rest, and fever control.

Prevention:
Avoid mosquito bites, remove stagnant water,
and use mosquito protection methods.
